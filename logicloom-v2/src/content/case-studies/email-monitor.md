## How The Logic Works
We built a **"Traffic Controller"** for your inbox. It reads every email instantly and puts it in the right pile.

1.  **Scan:** As soon as an email hits your inbox, our system reads it.
2.  **Decide:** It determines if it's a **Lead**, a **Complaint**, **Spam**, or a **Bill**.
3.  **Sort:**
    * *Spam?* Delete it.
    * *Urgent?* text you immediately.
    * *Routine?* Draft a reply and leave it in your drafts folder for approval.
4.  **Peace of Mind:** You only look at the emails that actually make you money.
